Dataset 2: South Australia Operational Regions

Dataset title: South Australia Operational Regions (Processed Spatial Dataset)

Dataset description: This processed spatial dataset was derived from the South Australian Government Regions dataset published by the South Australian Government [@sa_gov_regions].

The dataset contains geographic boundaries aggregated to approximate SA Water operational regions. It was created to support visualisation of regional differences in tap water characteristics across South Australia.

Purpose: This dataset was created to support mapping, visualisation, and regional comparison of tap water characteristics across South Australia.

Data source:
- Publisher: Department for Housing and Urban Development, Government of South Australia
- Platform: South Australian Government Open Data Portal
- Original dataset: South Australian Government Regions

Data collection method: The original dataset is administrative geographic boundary data used for government planning, monitoring, and service delivery across South Australia.

Processing steps: The original shapefile was processed by:
- Importing the GDA2020 version of the South Australian Government Regions shapefile.
- Creating a correspondence table between South Australian Government regions and SA Water operational region.
- Joining the correspondence table to the spatial dataset.
- Aggregating regional boundaries into six operational regions: Metropolitan, Outer Metro, Eyre, Northern, South East, and Remote Communities.
- Saving the resulting spatial dataset as an RDS file.

Spatial coverage: South Australia

Coordinate reference system (CRS): GDA2020

File format: RDS (spatial sf object)

Dataset dimensions:
- Rows: 6
- Columns: 2
- Geometry type: MULTIPOLYGON

Variables:
| Variable       | Description                                                         |
| -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| `water_region` | Approximate SA Water operational region used in the analysis.       |
| `geometry`     | MULTIPOLYGON geometry representing the operational region boundary. |


Region correspondence:
| SA Water Operational Region | Matched South Australian Government Regions                                 |
| --------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| Metropolitan                | Eastern Adelaide, Northern Adelaide, Southern Adelaide, Western Adelaide    |
| Outer Metro                 | Adelaide Hills, Barossa Light and Lower North, Fleurieu and Kangaroo Island |
| Eyre                        | Eyre and Western                                                            |
| Northern                    | Yorke and Mid North, Far North                                              |
| South East                  | Limestone Coast                                                             |
| Remote Communities          | Murray and Mallee                                                           |

Limitations:
- SA Water operational regions are not publicly available as spatial boundaries.
- Operational regions were approximated using South Australian Government regional boundaries.
- The correspondence table was created manually based on geographic location and SA Water service network information.
- The dataset is intended for exploratory visualisation and does not represent official SA Water operational boundaries.
